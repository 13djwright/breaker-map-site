Requirements

Breaker Map needs a LiDAR-equipped device. That means iPhone 12 Pro or newer (Pro models only), or any iPad Pro from 2020 onward. The 3D scanner is essential to placing electrical components accurately in space.

Pro upgrade

Breaker Map is free to download and use for a single home. The Pro upgrade is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited homes and panels, the printable PDF blueprint, Test Mode, and reference panel photos. Pro supports Family Sharing.

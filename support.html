Support

Need help with Breaker Map? Start with the answers below, and if you don't find what you need, get in touch.

System requirements

Breaker Map needs a LiDAR-equipped device. Compatible hardware:

  • iPhone 12 Pro, 13 Pro, 14 Pro, 15 Pro, 16 Pro, 17 Pro (Pro and Pro Max models)
  • iPad Pro 11" (2020) or newer
  • iPad Pro 12.9" (2020) or newer

Standard iPhones and non-Pro iPads do not include the LiDAR scanner the app relies on. The app will show a friendly explainer screen if it detects unsupported hardware.

Getting started

  1. Add your first home from the main screen.
  2. Tap Scan a Room and walk slowly through the room with your device, following the on-screen prompts. RoomPlan will build a 3D model as you go.
  3. Once the scan is done, tap to place markers for outlets, switches, lights, and any other electrical components you want to track.
  4. Open the Panel tab and configure your breaker panel — column count, slot count, doubles and tandems, optional reference photo.
  5. Assign each marker to its breaker so you have a complete circuit map.
  6. Optional: export a printable PDF from the Panel screen and stick it inside your electrical box.

Frequently asked questions

  • Why does the app say my device isn't supported?

    Breaker Map uses Apple's RoomPlan framework, which requires the LiDAR scanner found in Pro-tier iPhones and recent iPad Pros. Without LiDAR the app cannot place markers accurately in 3D space, so we block scanning on those devices rather than offering a degraded experience.

  • What does the Pro upgrade unlock?

    Pro unlocks unlimited additional homes and the printable PDF panel blueprint. It's a one-time purchase — no subscription — and it supports Family Sharing so you can share Pro with everyone in your iCloud Family group. The free tier already includes unlimited panels per home, full circuit assignment, Test Mode, and reference panel photos.

  • How do I restore a previous Pro purchase?

    Open Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. This will pull in any Pro unlock associated with your Apple ID, including purchases on another device and entitlements received through Family Sharing.

  • Can I share my Pro upgrade with my family?

    Yes. The Pro upgrade is configured for Family Sharing. Anyone in your iCloud Family group can install Breaker Map, tap Restore Purchases, and gain Pro features at no extra cost.

  • Where is my data stored?

    Everything lives locally on your device. Breaker Map does not have servers and does not sync your data anywhere. If you have iOS backups turned on, your data may be included in your encrypted Apple backup — see the Privacy Policy for details.

  • How do I back up or move my data to another device?

    From a home's detail screen, choose Export to save a .breakermap file. Open that file on another device with Breaker Map installed to import it. This is also a good way to keep an off-device archive of your mapping work.

  • How do I delete all my data?

    Delete individual homes or rooms from inside the app, or uninstall Breaker Map to remove every piece of its local data. Reinstalling starts fresh.

  • Can I rename or recolor things later?

    Yes — every marker, room, home, panel, and circuit can be renamed or re-edited at any time. The setup steps above don't lock anything in.

  • The icon switcher in Settings doesn't work — what's wrong?

    Make sure you're running the latest version. If the issue persists, contact us at the email below.

Contact

For anything not covered above — bug reports, feature requests, App Store issues, or general questions — reach out at breakermap.support@gmail.com. Please include your device model and iOS version when reporting a problem; it makes diagnosis a lot faster.

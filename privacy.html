Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

Short version: Breaker Map doesn't collect or transmit any of your data. Your 3D scans, photos, electrical layouts, and notes stay on your device. There are no analytics, no advertising trackers, no third-party SDKs, and no servers operated by Breaker Map.

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to the Breaker Map iOS app, distributed through the Apple App Store. Breaker Map is built and maintained by Devin Wright as an independent developer.

What data we collect

None. Breaker Map does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server controlled by us. The app has no account system, no login, no analytics, no crash reporting beyond Apple's built-in mechanisms, and no third-party integrations of any kind.

What stays on your device

When you use Breaker Map, the following information is created and stored locally on your iPhone or iPad:

  • 3D scans of the rooms you choose to scan, captured using Apple's RoomPlan framework.
  • The locations and types of the electrical components you pin (outlets, switches, lights, breakers, etc.) along with any notes or labels you add.
  • Your breaker panel layout, including circuit assignments and circuit metadata.
  • Photos you choose to attach as a reference for your breaker panel.
  • Settings such as your measurement unit preference and chosen app icon.

All of the above is stored in Breaker Map's private storage on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and the app's sandboxed Application Support directory. None of it is sent to us or to any third party.

Permissions the app uses

Camera and LiDAR scanner

The app uses your device's camera and LiDAR scanner to build a 3D model of the room you are scanning. The camera feed is processed entirely on-device by Apple's RoomPlan and ARKit frameworks. No camera frames or scan data are transmitted off your device by Breaker Map.

Photo library

If you choose to attach a reference photo to a breaker panel, the app uses Apple's photo picker to let you select an image. Only the specific photo you pick is read; Breaker Map does not request or receive access to your full photo library.

iCloud and device backups

If you have iCloud Backup or device backup enabled on your iPhone or iPad, the app's local data may be included in your encrypted Apple backup. That backup is handled entirely by Apple's iCloud service under Apple's privacy policy. Breaker Map does not operate any cloud sync of its own.

In-App Purchases

The Breaker Map Pro upgrade is handled by Apple's StoreKit. The transaction takes place between you and Apple. Breaker Map receives only the receipt information needed to verify that you have purchased Pro on your Apple ID, and that information is processed locally on your device.

Children's privacy

Breaker Map is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information about children. Since the app collects no data from anyone, no child-specific data collection or use occurs.

Your data, your control

Because everything Breaker Map creates lives on your device, you control it directly. You can:

  • Delete individual homes, rooms, panels, or markers inside the app.
  • Export a home as a .breakermap file to keep an off-device backup or move it to another device.
  • Uninstall the app, which deletes every piece of Breaker Map data on your device.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Breaker Map handles data, we will update this page and revise the effective date above. Material changes will also be reflected in the app's App Store listing.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to breakermap.support@gmail.com.
